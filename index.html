Fairy Bay Hotel is the 3 - star hotel in Nha Trang, Vietnam, located on the main beachfront and minutes from the heart of the Nha Trang city, is an ideal vacation paradise for adults and kids alike. Plenty of activities will keep you busy.

Fairy Bay Hotel makes boast of 52 rooms and Suites that combine irresistible, dynamic features with a contemporary interior design. A stunning, breathtaking views of the sunset, sunrise over Nha Trang Bay and the city from your private balconies and luxury amenities such as wireless Internet access and 24-hour room service.

The Ocean View Restaurant is located on the 10th Floor, here you will find well-known breakfast buffets, a variety of restaurants specializing in regional cuisine, cafes and bars and constant innovation in search of the latest taste sensations and culinary techniques.

Ocean View Restaurant offers fine dining for all tastes, whether you decide to enjoy a meal in the open air with ocean views or satisfy your taste buds with exquisite international dishes of European, Asian, seafood specialties as well as Vietnamese. The wonderful selection of hotel restaurants and bars means that choosing a place to eat becomes an adventure of exploration and discovery.
